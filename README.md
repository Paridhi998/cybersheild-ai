# 🔐 CyberShield AI

CyberShield AI is a Digital Asset Protection System designed to detect suspicious messages and simulate cyber attacks to spread cybersecurity awareness.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🔍 Detects phishing and suspicious messages  
- ⚠️ Alerts users about potential cyber threats  
- 🧪 Simulates common cyber attacks for learning  
- 💡 Simple and user-friendly interface  

---

- JavaScript

---

## 📌 How to Use

1. Enter a message or suspicious link  
2. Click on **Scan**  
3. Get instant result (Safe / Suspicious)  

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many users are unaware of phishing messages and online scams. This leads to loss of personal data and money.

---

## 💡 Solution

CyberShield AI helps users identify suspicious content and understand cyber threats through real-time scanning and simulation.
